How it works

75 days. Six things a day. Do them, tick them, watch yourself change.

1

Each day, tick your six: workout, protein, 3L water, 7+ hours sleep, daily prayer, and read a chapter.

2

Check in: add a photo and log your weight and waist. Daily or weekly, your choice.

3

Watch your dashboard: your trends, your streak, and the 75 day grid fill in.

4

Back each other: the crew leaderboard keeps everyone honest and moving.

5

Miss a day? Mark it and keep going. Tap a past day on the grid to fix it. No restart, just don't quit.
